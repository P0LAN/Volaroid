Volaroid

Volaroid is a monitoring device used in homes or a at an industrial enviroment. It can be used to detect harmful stuff in your work space or house like gas pollution and can detect fire

.

equipement

Voloroid is equipped with a mq3 gas sensor, dht22 temp sensor , analog fire sensor to monitor your enviroment precisly, it can detect harmful gases and fire outbrakes accurtly

.

Aim Of Volaroid

Volaroids aim is to make an iot device that YOUcontrol, that means you have %100 control of your DATA

.
